About Us

HairWorld is an online based, recognized hair brand concerned with the sales of quality hairs and hair-making services in Nigeria, with wholesale distributors of hair products in PortHarcourt, Lagos, Abuja, Akwa-Ibom, Ibadan, Delta, Imo and Abia states in Nigeria. With our highly sophisticated team of hair experts and stylists, and over 15 years of industry experience, HairWorld provides quality and affordable hairs and hair-making services. Our goal is to provide anyone with consistent access to a variety of beautiful hairstyles and hair products that are versatile, stylish and long-lasting. Utilising a highly meticulous business framework, HairWorld has always had a passion for creating bonds with clients and built the brand on the values of high quality, durability and consumer satisfaction in providing an incredible range of hair products so any and every single person who wishes to have access to affordable, beautiful hair styles and hair products can do so. We at HairWorld are devoted to developing and implementing new hairstyles in all our branches nationwide to meet the needs of our clients and to ensure every woman feels beautiful. It is our pride and joy to launch our online store offering retail purchases of various types of hair extensions and hair-making services.
